# 📊 Sales Trend Analysis Dashboard (Integrated from multiple sources)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project showcases an **interactive Power BI dashboard** focused on analyzing **sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance**. It provides a comprehensive view of revenue distribution across categories, cities, and time periods to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📌 Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** 10,296
* **Total Orders:** 150
* **Total Customers:** 150
* **Average Order Value:** 42.80K

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 1. Sales Trend Over Time

* Monthly visualization of revenue trends
* Identifies peak and low-performing months
* Helps in understanding seasonality in sales

---

### 🔹 2. Sales by Category

* Comparison across major product categories:

  * Accessories
  * Fashion
  * Electronics
* Accessories contribute the highest share of revenue

---

### 🔹 3. Top 10 Products by Revenue

* Highlights best-performing products
* Headphones and Watches lead in revenue generation
* Enables identification of high-demand products

---

### 🔹 4. Customer Purchase Behaviour

* Scatter plot analysis of:

  * Quantity purchased
  * Total spending (price)
* Shows purchasing patterns across categories
* Accessories show higher overall spending

---

### 🔹 5. Top Cities by Revenue

* Revenue contribution by cities:

  * Delhi
  * Mumbai
  * Pune
  * Bangalore
  * Chennai
  * Hyderabad
* Helps identify strong regional markets

---

### 🔹 6. Interactive Filters

* Product-based filtering (Bag, Headphones, Laptop, Phone)
* Date-based filtering for detailed time analysis
* Enables dynamic and customized insights

---
